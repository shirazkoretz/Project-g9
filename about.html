About Us

The Marocookies Factory store was established in 1993 by Shalom and Ellis Lankri and it's located in Hedera.
There is one branch- the official store, that offers traditional and authentic Marocco cookies.
The cookies is made by hand in the store, there is a veriaty of cookies by size and kind.

Shalom and Ellis born in Marocco and immigrated to Israel in 1962, have an affinity for Moroccan culture.
Therfore, the store aims to give the best Marrocan experiance and taste.

The store offer speical trays for events- order in advanced.
We also offer a veriaty of cookies made and sold in the store.
